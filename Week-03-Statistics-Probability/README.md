# Week 3: Statistics & Probability

## Overview

During Week 3 of the AnalystLab Africa Data Science Internship, I applied statistical analysis techniques to the cleaned Titanic and Housing Price datasets. The objective was to understand the characteristics of the data, evaluate relationships between variables, and draw statistically supported conclusions using Python.

## Objectives

* Compute descriptive statistics for numerical variables.
* Analyze probability distributions using visualizations.
* Perform hypothesis testing using independent two-sample t-tests.
* Examine relationships between variables through correlation analysis.
* Interpret statistical results in the context of real-world scenarios.

## Datasets

* **Titanic Dataset** – Passenger demographics and survival information.
* **Housing Price Dataset** – Residential property characteristics and sale prices.

## Statistical Techniques Applied

### Descriptive Statistics

* Mean

* Range

### Probability Distributions

* Statistical decision making

### Correlation Analysis

* Correlation Matrix
* Heatmap Visualization
* Scatter Plots
* Correlation vs. Causation
